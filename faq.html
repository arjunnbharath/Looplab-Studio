Orders & shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard delivery is 3–5 business days within the continental US. Express options appear at checkout when available for your address.

Do you offer free shipping?

Complimentary standard shipping applies on qualifying orders. The threshold is shown in your cart before checkout.

Can I change or cancel an order?

Contact us within one hour of placing your order. Once a package is packed we can’t change items, but we can help with returns when it arrives.

Returns & exchanges

What is your return window?

Unworn items with tags attached may be returned within 30 days of delivery. Final-sale pieces are marked on the product page and at checkout.

How do I start a return?

Visit Support and choose “Start a return,” or email hello@looplabstudio.com with your order number.

Account & loyalty

How do LoopLab Rewards points work?

Signed-in members earn points on purchases. Redeem in blocks of 100 points for $10 off in your cart. See Documentation for full program details.

I forgot my password

On this demo site, use the seed accounts in documentation or register a new account. Production sites should enable password reset email.
