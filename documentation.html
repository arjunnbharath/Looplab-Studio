Shopping & checkout

Browse departments from the main navigation or use search. Product detail pages support size selection and add-to-bag. Your bag is saved in this browser until you clear site data.

Checkout flow

  1. Add items from any product listing or PDP.
  2. Open Cart to review quantities, promos, and loyalty redemption.
  3. Choose Proceed to checkout to open the bill on Checkout.

Checkout is a demo—no payment is processed. Connect Stripe or your PSP when you go live.

LoopLab Rewards

Sign in to see your points balance in the header. Redeem points on the cart page in blocks of 100 pts = $10 off.

  • Guests cannot redeem points.
  • Redemption is stored in session storage for this demo.
  • Seed accounts ship with sample balances—see Accounts below.

Developer notes

Navigation and footer are shared partials loaded at runtime. Product catalog lives in data/products.json with a bundled copy for offline use.

Key paths

  • partials/page-nav.html — header & mega menus
  • partials/footer.html — site footer
  • js/page-cart-store.js — cart persistence
  • NAV.md / FOOTER.md — integration docs
